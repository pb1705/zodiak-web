# Logo Color & Font Guide for Zodiak

## Exact Site Background Color

**Primary Background:** `#0F172A` (Slate 900 - Dark Navy/Slate)

This is a deep, rich dark blue-gray that provides excellent contrast for light elements and creates a premium, cosmic aesthetic.

## Logo Color Recommendations for `#0F172A` Background

Based on WCAG contrast requirements and modern design trends, here are optimal logo colors:

### ✅ **Recommended Colors (High Contrast & Premium Feel)**

1. **Pure White** - `#FFFFFF`
   - Contrast Ratio: 15.8:1 (AAA)
   - Best for: Clean, minimal logos
   - Works with: All logo styles

2. **Trust Blue** - `#63B3ED` (Current accent color)
   - Contrast Ratio: 4.8:1 (AA)
   - Best for: Modern, tech-forward feel
   - Works with: Geometric, minimalist logos

3. **Light Blue** - `#7DD3FC` (Current light accent)
   - Contrast Ratio: 5.2:1 (AA)
   - Best for: Softer, approachable feel
   - Works with: Organic, flowing logos

4. **Gold/Amber** - `#F59E0B` (Current energy color)
   - Contrast Ratio: 5.1:1 (AA)
   - Best for: Luxury, premium feel
   - Works with: Elegant, sophisticated logos

5. **Premium Indigo** - `#818CF8` (Current premium color)
   - Contrast Ratio: 4.5:1 (AA)
   - Best for: Mystical, spiritual feel
   - Works with: Celestial, cosmic logos

### 🎨 **Gradient Options (Premium Look)**

- **White to Blue Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #FFFFFF 0%, #63B3ED 100%)`
- **Gold to White Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #F59E0B 0%, #FFFFFF 100%)`
- **Blue to Indigo Gradient**: `linear-gradient(135deg, #63B3ED 0%, #818CF8 100%)`

### ⚠️ **Colors to Avoid**

- Very dark colors (won't show on dark background)
- Low contrast colors (below 4.5:1 ratio)
- Pure black (no contrast with #0F172A)

## Font Research for Logo Typography

### **2025 Award-Winning Logo Font Trends**

Based on research of premium brands and award-winning designs:

#### **1. Modern Sans-Serif (Tech/Luxury)**
- **Inter** - Clean, geometric, highly legible
- **Poppins** - Friendly yet professional
- **Space Grotesk** - Futuristic, modern
- **Outfit** - Contemporary, approachable
- **DM Sans** - Versatile, clean

#### **2. Elegant Serif (Premium/Luxury)**
- **Cormorant Garamond** - Currently used, elegant serif
- **Playfair Display** - Dramatic, sophisticated
- **EB Garamond** - Classic, refined
- **Bodoni Moda** - High-fashion, editorial
- **Cinzel** - Ancient, mystical (perfect for astrology)

#### **3. Display/Decorative (Unique Identity)**
- **Bebas Neue** - Bold, impactful
- **Oswald** - Condensed, strong
- **Montserrat** - Modern, geometric
- **Raleway** - Elegant, thin weights available

### **Font Pairing Recommendations**

**Option 1: Premium Minimal**
- Logo: **Cormorant Garamond** (serif, elegant)
- Body: **Inter** (sans-serif, clean)
- *Current setup - excellent choice*

**Option 2: Modern Tech**
- Logo: **Space Grotesk** or **Outfit** (sans-serif, modern)
- Body: **Inter** (sans-serif, consistent)

**Option 3: Mystical Premium**
- Logo: **Cinzel** or **Playfair Display** (serif, dramatic)
- Body: **Inter** or **Poppins** (sans-serif, readable)

### **Logo Typography Best Practices**

1. **Weight**: Use 300-400 for elegance, 600-700 for impact
2. **Letter Spacing**: Increase tracking (0.1em - 0.3em) for premium feel
3. **Size**: Ensure readability at small sizes (favicon, mobile)
4. **Contrast**: Maintain 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
5. **Simplicity**: Keep logo text simple, avoid decorative fonts for small sizes
